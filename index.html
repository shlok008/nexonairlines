Our Services

Services for our members

Virtual Flights

Fly to destinations across the globe in our state-of-the-art virtual aircraft. From short regional hops to long-haul international flights, we offer a diverse range of routes to satisfy your wanderlust.

Flight Training

Whether you're a seasoned virtual pilot or just starting your aviation journey, we provide comprehensive flight training programs tailored to your skill level. Learn the ropes, enhance your knowledge, and master the art of flying with our experienced instructors.

Community Engagement

At Nexon Airlines, we believe in fostering a vibrant and supportive community. Connect with fellow aviation enthusiasts through our forums, social media channels, and organized events. Share your experiences, exchange tips and tricks, and build lasting friendships with like-minded individuals.

Virtual Rewards

Earn virtual rewards and unlock achievements as you progress through your virtual pilot career. From earning prestigious badges for completing challenging flights to unlocking new aircraft models, our reward system adds an extra layer of excitement and motivation to your virtual aviation journey.

Virtual Cargo Operations

Expand your virtual aviation experience by participating in our cargo operations. Transport goods across the globe, manage logistics, and experience the thrill of operating a successful cargo airline.

Fleet

Explore our impressive fleet of virtual aircraft, meticulously designed to replicate real-world counterparts. From modern passenger jets to classic vintage planes, Nexon Airlines offers a diverse range of aircraft for you to choose from for your virtual flights.

Virtual Cabin Experience

Immerse yourself in the virtual cabin environment with stunning visuals, realistic sounds, and attention to detail. Experience the thrill of takeoffs, landings, and in-flight services, creating an authentic airline experience from the comfort of your own home.

Flight Scheduling:

Stay up-to-date with our comprehensive flight scheduling system. Access our easy-to-use online platform to view available flights, plan your itineraries, and reserve your virtual flights in advance.

Virtual Airline Partnerships:

Nexon Airlines has established partnerships with other virtual airlines and organizations, allowing for codeshare agreements and collaborative events. Take advantage of our network and explore even more destinations and opportunities.

Virtual Crew Positions:

If you're looking to take your virtual aviation experience to the next level, consider joining our virtual crew. We offer opportunities for virtual pilots, flight attendants, and other roles, allowing you to immerse yourself in various aspects of the airline industry.

Events and Contests

Stay engaged and test your skills by participating in our regular events and contests. From flight challenges to landing competitions, these activities provide an extra layer of excitement and friendly competition within the Nexon Airlines community.

Support and Assistance

Our dedicated support team is always ready to assist you with any inquiries, technical issues, or general assistance you may need. Feel free to reach out to us through our contact channels, and we'll be happy to help.
